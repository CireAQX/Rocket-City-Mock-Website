Key Terms

  • OVR (Overall Rating) - A player's overall skill rating in the game, from 0 to 99.
  • Dynasty Mode - A game mode where you control a college football program across multiple seasons.
  • Redshirt - Holding a player out of games for a season to preserve a year of eligibility.
  • Depth Chart - The ranked list of players at each position from starter to backup.
  • Development Trait - A trait that determines how fast a player improves over time.
  • Recruiting Class - The group of incoming players signed during a recruiting cycle.
