About This Project

The Rocket City Moon Men concept is not an original creation. The team name, primary logo, secondary logo, and uniform designs were originally created by the YouTuber NotTheExpert for an "NCAA Football 14" TeamBuilder dynasty series on YouTube. All original credit for the team identity, branding, and visual design belongs to Not The Expert.

For this project, I recreated the logos in Adobe Illustrator and rebuilt the team, uniforms, and roster into the modern collegiate football video game of "College Football 26 (CFB26)", all based on the original YouTube series. All in-game screenshots (Any pciture of a specific player) gameplay data, and the home page banner (Made from Adobe Illustrator) featured on this website were captured from my own gameplay. The logos and team identity are used here with acknowledgment of the original creator.

This website is strictly for academic purposes as part of the IT 3203 Web Development course and is not intended for commercial use, distribution, or any purpose beyond this class assignment.
